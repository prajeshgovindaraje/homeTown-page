Discover Pondy

A colony in the southern cost of India

Places to visit in Pondy

Beaches

Promenade Beach, located in the heart of the city, is perfect for leisurely walks along the coast, with its scenic views and colonial-era landmarks like the War Memorial and Gandhi statue. A short boat ride away lies Paradise Beach, famous for its pristine golden sands and calm waters, making it ideal for a peaceful getaway.

Basillica Church

The Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Jesus in Pondicherry is one of the most prominent and beautiful churches in the region, showcasing a fine example of Gothic architecture. Built by French missionaries in the early 20th century, the basilica is adorned with stunning stained-glass panels depicting scenes from the life of Jesus and saints.

Auroville

Auroville, located in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu, is an experimental township that embodies the spirit of universal harmony and sustainable living. Founded in 1968, Auroville aims to be a place where people from all over the world can live together in peace and unity, transcending national, cultural, and religious boundaries.

Activities to do in Pondy

Marina Lighthouse

It's one of the best spots to get a breathtaking view of the coastline and the Bay of Bengal. The lighthouse is just a short walk from Promenade Beach, so it’s super convenient to reach. Once you're at the top, you'll get a stunning panoramic view of the city and the ocean, especially during sunset – it's an experience you won’t want to miss.

Walking by White Town

While you're in Pondicherry, you definitely have to take a stroll through White Town. It’s the heart of the city’s French Quarter and feels like stepping into a different world with its beautiful colonial architecture.

Boating

Located just a short drive from Pondicherry, Ousteri is a tranquil freshwater lake surrounded by lush greenery, and it's the perfect spot for nature lovers. The best way to enjoy the lake is by taking a boat ride – it's incredibly calming and offers beautiful views of the water and the surrounding wetlands.
